Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first ride.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Just go to your dashboard and click "Cancel Booking". Full refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

What is covered by the insurance?

All rentals include comprehensive third-party liability and collision damage waiver (CDW). A security deposit is held but fully refundable upon safe return of the vehicle.

Do I need a special license to rent?

You just need a valid driver's license that has been held for at least 1 year (or 3 years for premium/luxury vehicles). International licenses are accepted for non-residents.

Are there any hidden fees?

Never! The price you see is the price you pay. Taxes, airport fees, and standard insurance are included. The only extra is a fully refundable security deposit.
